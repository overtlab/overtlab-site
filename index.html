Contemporary power operates overtly.
Overt Lab is an investigative design studio based in Montréal. We assemble publicly available signals into spatial and structural evidence — mapping the flows, networks, and decisions that shape public life in Québec and beyond.

We do not accuse. We do not editorialize. We map structures and let people draw their own conclusions.
